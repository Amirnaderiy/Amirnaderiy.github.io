I'm Amir Reza Naderi. Born and raised in Tehran, I have a Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering from Islamic Azad University Science and Research Branch. My research interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Computer Vision.

I am proficient in Python and MATLAB programming languages and have hands-on experience in both. I am now seeking new opportunities to continue my academic journey and pursue a PhD degree.

With my educational background and expertise in the fields of Machine Learning and Computer Vision, I am eager to contribute to cutting-edge research and tackle complex problems in these areas.
